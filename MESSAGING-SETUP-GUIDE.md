# Grimace Remote: Mobile Messaging Setup Guide

To reach "Mobile Mastery," you should enable direct messaging on your business profiles. This allows customers to "Text" you directly from Google Search or Facebook.

---

## 1. Google Business Messages (Instant GMB Texting)
This puts a "MESSAGING" button on your Google Maps listing.
1.  Open the **Google Maps** app on your phone.
2.  Tap **Business** -> **Messages** -> **Settings**.
3.  Turn on **Messaging**.
4.  **Edit your Welcome Message**: 
    *"Hi! Thanks for reaching out to Grimace Remote. Send us a message about your tech issue and we'll reply shortly. For emergencies, call 0407 464 371."*

---

## 2. Facebook Messenger Integration
Turn your Facebook page into a text-hub.
1.  Go to your **Facebook Business Page**.
2.  Go to **Professional Dashboard** -> **Messaging Settings**.
3.  Enable **"Show a greeting"**.
4.  Download the **Meta Business Suite** app to receive these messages as instant notifications (just like a text).

---

## 3. The "Direct Text" Advantage
On your website, you now have `sms:` links. 
- When a user on an iPhone or Android taps "Text an Expert," it opens their default messaging app with your number ready to go.
- **Goal**: Respond within 5-10 minutes. Speed is the #1 factor in closing leads from mobile devices.
